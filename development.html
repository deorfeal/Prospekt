Our Development Stack

At Prospekt, we select the most appropriate technology for each project, offering solutions from CMS platforms like WordPress and ModX, to custom frameworks such as React and Laravel — always ensuring optimal performance and alignment with project objectives. Front-end is crafted with precision using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for adaptive, responsive, and pixel-perfect layouts. Back-end solutions are built to be robust, scalable, and maintainable, tailored to the specific business logic of each client. We rely exclusively on licensed, industry-standard software and development tools to guarantee reliability, security, and long-term support. Every decision in our stack is guided by a focus on performance, usability, and delivering a digital product that maximizes value and strategic impact.

Step 1:
Discovery & Project Briefing
(Assessment)

We start by gathering all essential information about your business, goals, audience, and technical requirements. This ensures a clear understanding of your objectives and forms the foundation for every decision throughout the project.
By aligning early, we set the stage for a precise, strategic, and efficient development process.

Step 2:
Planning & Workplan Approval
(Alignment)

We present a comprehensive project roadmap, including milestones, deliverables, and timelines. This collaborative session allows you to provide feedback, refine priorities, and approve the plan before development begins. It ensures transparency, resource alignment, and full confidence in the project’s execution.

Step 3:
Architecture & UX Prototyping
(Blueprint)

Based on your requirements, we create detailed site architecture, user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes.
Navigation, content hierarchy, and user scenarios are carefully mapped to optimize usability and business efficiency. This step allows early validation of structure and functionality before development starts.

Step 4:
Front-End & Visual Integration
(Interface)

Our team implements the design into functional interfaces, ensuring the layout, typography, and visual identity are pixel-perfect. All adaptive and responsive behaviors are addressed, guaranteeing seamless experience across devices and resolutions. Interactive elements, animations, and micro-interactions are integrated to enhance usability and engagement.

Step 5:
Back-End Development & System Implementation
(Infrastructure)

We build the server-side logic, databases, CMS integrations, and custom functionality as required. Every system is robust, scalable, and aligned with the business goals of the project.
We test performance, security, and cross-platform consistency throughout this stage to ensure a high-quality, reliable product.

Step 6:
Testing, Launch & Ongoing Support
(Delivery)

Before deployment, the site undergoes thorough QA testing: functionality, performance, security, and responsiveness. After launch, we provide full documentation, training, and post-release support to ensure long-term maintainability. This final stage guarantees your website is fully operational, optimized, and aligned with your strategic objectives.

Web Development Expertise Across Industries

Prospekt Studio delivers full-cycle web solutions tailored to diverse industries and business needs. Our experience spans luxury fashion, collectible watches, fine jewelry, consumer goods, electronics, and household products, as well as corporate sites for large organizations, event agencies, construction and architectural firms, automotive brands, and more. We design and develop promotional and product-focused landing pages, sales funnels, booking platforms, real estate portals, catalogs, medical and government services, and eCommerce solutions. Interactive and gamified projects are a specialty — from gaming marketplaces and 3D virtual exhibitions to AI-powered platforms and immersive web applications, enhanced with animations and micro-interactions to elevate user experience. Every project is executed with strategic precision, combining unique design, technical excellence, and interactive innovation to deliver websites that reflect your brand, engage users, and achieve measurable results.
