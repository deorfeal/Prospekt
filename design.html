Our Tools & Design Stack

We rely on a precise, modern design stack to ensure consistency, clarity, and production-ready output. Figma powers our core workflow — from UX architecture to UI design, prototyping, and collaborative review. For advanced visuals and motion, we work in Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects, creating custom illustrations, micro-interactions, and interface animations. Lottie is used for lightweight, high-performance animations that integrate smoothly into websites and applications.
Every tool in our ecosystem is selected for accuracy and seamless developer handoff — ensuring each design is visually refined, technically sound, and ready to perform.

Step 1:
Discovery & Project Briefing
(Insights)

We begin by gathering all essential information about your project: goals, target audience, brand values, and technical requirements. This stage ensures that we fully understand your business context and objectives before any design work begins. Every insight is documented, forming a foundation for strategic design decisions. By clarifying expectations early, we set a clear direction for the entire project.

Step 2:
Alignment & Workplan Approval
(Agreement)

Next, we meet with you to present a detailed workplan, timeline, and scope of deliverables. This collaborative session allows for discussion, adjustments, and agreement on priorities. We align on expectations, resources, and milestones to ensure transparency and avoid surprises. Your feedback at this stage guarantees that the project moves forward with full confidence and clarity.

Step 3:
UX Strategy & Prototyping
(Structure)

We translate your requirements into structured user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes. Scenarios, navigation paths, and information architecture are carefully mapped to create intuitive and efficient user experiences.
This stage allows us to test usability and validate functionality before visual design begins. Prototypes are shared for review, ensuring alignment with both user needs and business goals.

Step 4:
UI Concept & Visual Design
(Expression)

With UX validated, we craft the visual language of your product.
Layouts, color palettes, typography, iconography, and other visual elements are designed to reinforce your brand identity. We focus on creating interfaces that are visually distinct, cohesive, and communicate your unique value.
The result is a set of polished, high-fidelity screens that reflect strategy and aesthetics.

Step 5:
Responsive & Interactive Design
(Engagement)

Next, we adapt your UI for multiple devices and resolutions — mobile, tablet, desktop, and high-resolution displays. Interactive elements, animations, and micro-interactions are designed and tested to enhance usability and engagement.
This stage ensures a seamless experience across platforms, while maintaining visual consistency and brand identity. Every interaction is purposeful, reinforcing clarity and delight in user behavior.

Step 6:
Delivery & Developer Handoff
(Implementation)

Finally, we prepare all assets for implementation: responsive layouts, UI kits, style guides, and design specifications. All files are organized and optimized for seamless handoff to the development team. This ensures that your website or app is built precisely as designed, without compromises. We document components, interactions, and guidelines so that future updates remain consistent and efficient.
